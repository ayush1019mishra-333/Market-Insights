# 📊 Market Insights & Vendor's Performance Dashboard

This project focuses on turning raw business data into meaningful market insights that can support smarter decision-making.  
Using **Python, MySQL, and Power BI**, I worked on analyzing sales trends, vendor performance, inventory movement, and profitability to understand how the business is performing over time.

The main objective of this project was to move beyond just numbers and create a dashboard that tells a clear business story through data.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze retail sales and inventory data to identify:

- overall sales performance
- revenue and profit trends
- vendor-wise contribution
- unsold inventory
- stock movement and inventory efficiency
- high-performing and low-performing products

This dashboard helps in understanding market behavior and business growth patterns.
